The pipeline

From report to responder, one continuous line.

Signal follows a report through three fixed stages — nothing is dropped, and every stage is visible on the map in real time.

01 — INTAKE

A report comes in

Calls, SMS, and app submissions land in one queue, tagged with location and urgency the moment they arrive.

02 — MAP

It appears on the live map

Signal plots the incident instantly, next to every available responder, so dispatchers see the whole picture at once.

03 — DISPATCH

Nearest responder is sent

The closest available team is routed in, with live position tracking until the report is marked resolved.
